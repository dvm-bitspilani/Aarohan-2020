APOGEE

After round 1 , Aarohan gives each state and city topper a certificate of merit stating the same . The national winners of Round 1 are then invited to APOGEE ,Bits Pilani for round 2 of Aarohan which is conducted in the BITS Pilani , Pilani campus itself.

APOGEE is the first ISO 9001:2008 certified technical fest in India . This is a hub of the brightest minds of the country. APOGEE enjoys a footfall of over 8000 students from more than 100 colleges nationwide. Dr A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, Mugdha Saini, Danielle Feinberg, Simon Taufel, Stephen P Morse, Rakesh Sharma and many more distinguished personalities have been a part of APOGEE.

Technical Events like Full Throttle Grand Prix, Indian Navy Demo, IDRL (Indian Drone Race League), International Coding League, Robots-at-War, and many more, sum up to 80+ events which steer to challenge the intellect of the participants and piques the minds of the audience.

Events

Aarohan round 2 is conducted during APOGEE. It conducts exclusive events for the students selected for round 2. Aarohan has seen a few events like trivia quiz , bob the builder , anti chess , infinity war and many more. Our round 2 participants get to meet the distinguished personalities who visit the campus during APOGEE.

Team Aarohan will give you detailed guidelines on how to reach BITS Pilani campus. Once you reach here along with other national winners, you will explore a plethora of technical events, workshops, exhibitions happening on the campus and we'll have Round-2 and some other events exclusively for you.

Team Aarohan and you have the same mission here, which is to make your experience here during Apogee the best and unforgivable. And yeah, we have some amazing goodies and certificates of merit for the winners and certificates of participation for all the students.

Some of the events in the previous editions are given below:

01

Visit to Innovation Hub

The Innovation Hub is a place that gives direction and shape to the creativity of young minds, in the form of technological support and info. At this Aarohan Round 2 event, students were succinctly introduced to electronics and IoT, and concluding this, they had to construct a robotic, sensor-based vehicle. After this, there was a demonstration of the Hub's brand new 3-D printer and the associated software.

02

Trivia Quiz

Do you think you're aware of your surroundings? Do you consider yourself "trivia master"? For such knowledge enthusiasts, for whom knowing about anything and everything is the most important, we have Trivia Quiz. An adrenaline driven quizzing event spread over various rounds, which not only test the knowledge in science, but also the general awareness and the ability to be ahead of the competition by being the first to answer. The participants put on your thinking caps as they answer their way to victory.

03

Infinity War

Infinity war, the card game involving all 6 infinity stones each with their special abilities obtained by solving a particular set of problems to boost the powers of the cards obtained via an auction. This was designed to make the participants learn and solve questions whilst having to dive into the Marvel cosmic universe,which included using the infinity stones in a 1v1 battle and break free from the monotony of regular quizzes and approach evaluations with a whole new outlook.
