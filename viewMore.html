AAROHAN

Aarohan is a national contest curated for classes 9th to 12th with an aim to inculcate scientific temperament in the minds of the young generation.

It is crafted and conducted by the students of BITS Pilani . Aarohan has been growing exponentially ever since its inception in 2012. What started off as a humble attempt to rekindle curiosity is now a nationwide event. We also made a mark at the international stage with participation from Abu Dhabi and Germany.

With this paper, we are trying to revive the lost essence of real education which involves questioning the very fundamentals of any phenomenon.

PATTERN

01

Round 1 - Online

The paper will be multiple choice based , duration of the paper being two hours.

The paper will be based on a single theme based on recent scientific phenomenon.

There are no prerequisites required to attempt the paper as the syllabus would be the same as the syllabus for the student’s respective class and it would be purely application based.

By the end of the paper the students would definitely have learned something new which would encourage them to question science and they would definitely start believing in the quote “Nothing in life is to be feared, it is to be understood. ”

Round 2 - On Campus

After Round 1, Aarohan gives each state and city topper a certificate of merit stating the same.

The national winners of Round 1 are then invited to APOGEE, BITS Pilani, for Round 2 of Aarohan, which is conducted in the BITS Pilani, Pilani campus.

Team Aarohan will give you detailed guidelines on how to reach the BITS Pilani campus. Once you reach here along with other national winners, you will explore a plethora of technical events, workshops, and exhibitions happening on the campus, and we'll have Round-2 and some other events exclusively for you.

Team Aarohan and you have the same mission: to make your experience here during Apogee the best and unforgettable. And yeah, we have some amazing goodies, certificates of merit for the winners, and certificates of participation for all the students

Some of the events in the previous editions are given below:

01

Infinity War

Infinity war is the card game involving all six infinity stones, each with their special abilities obtained by solving a particular set of problems to boost the powers of the cards obtained via an auction. This was designed to make the participants learn and solve questions whilst having to dive into the Marvel cosmic universe, which included using the infinity stones in a 1v1 battle and breaking free from the monotony of regular quizzes and approach evaluations with a whole new outlook.

02

Murder Mystery

Dive into the murder of Rameshwar Babu, a famous pharmaceutical tycoon and search his bungalow for clues along with the statements given by the suspects and try to figure out the murderer, the murder weapon and the motive. This was designed in such a way that students had to unravel all the clues in a team to find out the dark side of each of the suspects and then use their deduction skills to find out the real murderer. Every clue was different in its own way and the only way a team can crack this mystery was through teamwork.

03

Maze Runner

Maze Runner is a Scotland Yard spinoff where the detectives(students) have to find Mr X, who is hiding in his safe spot in the city. The detectives can move either using a Taxi(Easy), a Train(Medium) or Underground(Hard), each transport coming up with a challenge. The harder the challenge farther you can explore. Each challenge requires the detectives to think out of the box while having a hold of their basic concepts. Want to try some yourselves?
