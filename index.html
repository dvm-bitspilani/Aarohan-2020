About Us
Aarohan is a national level examination crafted and conducted by the students of BITS Pilani . The examination is conducted for the classes of IX ,X,XI and XIIth . It is conducted in two rounds . Round one comprises a written examination There are no prerequisites required to attempt the paper as the syllabus would be the same as the syllabus for the studentâ€™s respective class and it would be purely application based .The winners of round 1 are then invited to BITS pilani to be a part of the round 2 . The winners of round 2 win cash prizes and much more.

Round 2

After round 1 , the national winners of Aarohan are invited to APOGEE ,Bits Pilani for round 2 of Aarohan which is conducted in the BITS Pilani , Pilani campus itself. APOGEE is the annual, international technical festival of BITS Pilani. .Dr A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, Mugdha Saini, Danielle Feinberg, Simon Taufel, Stephen P Morse, Rakesh Sharma and many more distinguished personalities have been a part of APOGEE. The students who get selected for the round 2 get to meet these legendary personalities in person.

About Apogee
APOGEE, the international annual technical fest of BITS Pilani is one of the largest technological symposiums of the country.Truly justifying it's dictionary meaning as "the highest point", Apogee signifies the zenith of science, engineering and path-breaking technology in a landscape of future visionaries, scientists, corporate bigwigs and inventors. APOGEE enjoys a footfall of over 8000 students from more than 100 colleges nationwide.Dr A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, Mugdha Saini, Danielle Feinberg, Simon Taufel, Stephen P Morse, Rakesh Sharma and many more distinguished personalities have been a part of APOGEE. Frequently Asked Questions
Q:Who all are eligible for the exam?
Students of classes 9-10 and 11-12 (Science with Mathematics), studying in school affiliated with any board are eligible to appear in the exam.
Q:How will the exam be conducted?
The exam will be conducted in Online Mode. All the registered students will be getting their login credentials and Admit cards with the link for the online examination portal.
Q:What is the exam pattern?
The exam focuses on enriching the application skill-set of the students, rather than testing the memory. A set of passages with a unifying scientific phenomenon is provided, based on which MCQs are given. For sample papers, click on the link
Q:How credible is the paper, since itâ€™s designed by a student body?
The paper made by the organizing committee would be proof-read by a panel of Professors and the credibility of the paper is unquestionable.
Q:What would the students gain?
The paper aims to inculcate scientific temperament and evoke the curiosity of a student. It requires no preparation and is completely IQ based. Regional winners get loads of goodies among other benefits and the national winners get to visit BITS Pilani during APOGEE-our international annual technical festival, with all events expenses taken care of.
Q:What is the registration process?
The students are required to register themselves on our website. If a school wants to register their students, they can reach out to us for registering the students on the behalf of the school.
Q:What is the exam/registration fee?
The exam fee is Rs 150 per student, to be paid online on our website.
Q:How is this different from other Olympiads?
The examination, unlike many others, doesn't stress on solving more problems in less time, but focuses more on helping increase the intellect and the problem-solving ability of the student.
